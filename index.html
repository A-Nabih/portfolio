About

Turning Ideas Into Powerful, Reliable Backend Systems

I'm Ahmed Nabih, a backend engineer with over 5 years of experience building the systems that keep products running smoothly behind the scenes — from clean REST APIs to full SaaS platforms and automation tooling.

I've delivered software across real estate, security, event management, logistics, and fitness, helping teams ship products that are fast, secure, and ready to scale. Most recently, I architected TJM Hub and the TJM Engine — a connected platform and desktop automation suite used to manage licensing, billing, and automations for clients.

Why Work With Me?
  • Scalable by design — backend systems that handle growth in traffic and data without breaking.
  • Security-first — authentication, access control, and data protection built in from day one.
  • End-to-end delivery — from API design and databases to containerized cloud deployment.
