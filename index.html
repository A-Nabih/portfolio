About

Turning Your Ideas into Powerful, Reliable Backend Solutions

Have you ever wondered what makes a great website or app work smoothly behind the scenes? That’s where I come in. I’m Ahmed Nabih, a Backend Engineer with over 4 years of experience in building secure and high-performing systems that make your business run like a well-oiled machine.

From developing simple applications to creating complex platforms, I focus on building the backend systems that make everything function smoothly, securely, and at scale. I’ve worked with businesses across different industries, such as real estate, event management, anti-counterfeiting, and fitness, ensuring their systems are fast, reliable, and capable of growing as their business does.

Why Choose Me?
  • Reliable & Scalable – I build backend systems that can handle increasing traffic and grow with your business needs.
  • Security-Focused – I prioritize protecting your data and ensuring your users have a secure experience.
  • Full Backend Solutions – Whether it's designing APIs, managing databases, or deploying to the cloud, I provide end-to-end solutions that cover all aspects of your backend.
